WHO ARE WE

CITE is short for “Connecting Individuals To Employment”, representing our mission to equip individuals with the vital skills and knowledge necessary to remain competitive in the corporate world today. Running on state-of-the-art technology, our online educational platform houses a library of resources that facilitates hassle-free and convenient learning.

ELEARNING

CITE offers users an e-learning experience specially tailored to their needs. Select from our wide range of courses available and pick up critical skills with our short yet informative lessons that are made easy to understand. The CITE library consists of content curated from in-house specialists, trusted external sources as well as exclusive content from industry experts.

ASSESSMENTS & CERTIFICATION

For a holistic approach to online learning, CITE provides users with quizzes along with the lessons in order to assess their level of understanding. Additionally, certifications from CITE and our partnered educational institutes are offered should you complete the prerequisites of certain courses.

BUSINESS

CITE for Businesses is an avenue for businesses to collaborate with CITE to create customized programs catered to their organizational needs.

CITE for BUSINESS

We provide a comprehensive and customizable corporate education platform for businesses to train their staff, accordingly to how they want it.
